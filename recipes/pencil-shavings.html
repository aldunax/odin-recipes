Healthy Pencil Shaving Soup

Description

This delicious soup is the perfect low calorie, low carb, low sodium alternative for your breakfast cereal cravings. Made of 30-50% natural ingredients too!

Need I say more?

Ingredients

  • Pencils, wood or colored
  • Milk
  • Pencil sharpener

Directions

  1. Sharpen pencils until the sharpener is filled completely.
  2. Empty shavings into bowl, repeat step 1 until desired volume of shavings is achieved.
  3. Pour milk into bowl.
  4. Voila!!!
