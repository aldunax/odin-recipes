The Ooeist Gooeist Chocolately Chip Cookies

Description

Bazinga!!! You're not gonna believe your eyes when these fresh cookies come out of the oven!

These are the yummiest, the ooiest, the GOOEIST cookies that you or your momma or your memaw or your pappy or your great memaw has ever seen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, soft as a baby's bottom
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, and pack it good
  • 2 eggs, unfertilized
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon soda, but the baking kind, not the other kind
  • 2 teaspoons of piping hot yummy water
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flowers
  • Actually, I've been told it's actually flour, not flowers, so sorry if you missed that note before.
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts from inside of your wall
  • Again, so sorry, I've now been informed that they're called walnuts and don't come from the walls.

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients. Why do I even have to tell you that? Seriously?
  2. Preheat your oven to 350 freedom degrees (or whatever your local equivalent of that is).
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together. But not too hard, just enough to send a message.
  4. Beat in eggs, one a time for your safety, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flowers, chocolate chips, and nuts of the wall.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Make sure not to put them too closely together, 2 inches is more than you'd think.
  8. Bake in the oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes depending on your elevation. Good luck figuring out how long it actually is supposed to be, because I sure haven't been able to.
  9. Let them cool so as not to burn your precious little fingies
  10. Profit!
